FAQ

1. Do I need a myanimelist.net account to use your recommender? 

 Yes, our recommender retrieves your rated and watched animes from your listings on myanimelist.net and generates a list of animes catered to your anime preferences.

2. Does your recommender alter my anime listing from my myanimelist.net account? 

 No, Otaku Hub Anime Recommender is read only so your myanimelist.net private information will not be accessed or affected.

3. Can anyone my anime recommendation list? 

 Yes, If a person knows your myanimelist.net username they will be able to generate recommendations based upon your preferences.

4. Does Otaku Hub Anime Recommender provide links to third party streaming sites? 

  No, Otaku Hub Recommender does not condone or enganage in the illegal distribution of media.

5. What types of animes will Otaku Hub Anime Recommender recommend me? 

Otaku Hub Anime Recommender will recommend you animes with an upcoming season. Animes recommended to you can range from animes getting their first season or popular animes getting their n-th season
