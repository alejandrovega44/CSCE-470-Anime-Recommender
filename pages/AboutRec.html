Otaku Hub Anime Recommender

Otaku Hub Anime Recommender is not your traditional anime recommender. Our anime recommender aims to provide you with animes with upcoming seasons. So how does our recommender work? Our recommender uses your myanimelist.net account and retrieves your anime listings and anime reviews to formulate an anime recommendation lisiting based on your anime preferences. Source code for this recommender can be found at github.com/alejandrovega44/CSCE-470-Anime-Recommender
